A blog post about the blog itself

Having a blog was my bucket list item for the last 4 years. The idea of a blog excites me because I see it as a journal & an information repository that I want to share with the world. Things like my opinions, side projects, experiments and how-tos based on what I have learned etc. will make its way into this blog.

Being a programmer, it is easy for me to buy a domain and set up a website, the hard part always been writing actual content. For at least some of you, it could be the other way around. My last two attempts at it - iamvivek.xyz & vivek-s.me, been failures in that aspect. This time I am determined not to make the same mistakes. So let's see, Third time's a charm, right?
