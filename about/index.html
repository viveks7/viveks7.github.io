About

Sunday, December 29, 2019

Hi, I am Vivek.

I am a developer based out of Kerala, India. Addicted to computers from a young age, I have been developing small micro-controller based programs to complex enterprise applications for some time now. I see technology as an “enabler” & was marvelled by the progress, we as a species, abled to achieve using it.

In the last 5 years, I have co-created two startups, Codeister Technologies & Spoon.Money. My first venture, Codeister Technologies, was started during my college days. Our product was an automated expense manager app called SYM (Save Your Money). During my final semester, our team was acqui-hired by a US-based company called CareStack. I was part of the CareStack's engineering team for around 9-months before deciding to quit and become a founding member of Spoon. At Spoon, we are on a mission to provide financial services to the next billion.

This blog is a journal of my opinions, my side projects and learnings.
